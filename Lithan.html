  • 2 main topics in this module: DAT203x (AML) and DAT209x (R Programming)

  • DAT203x (all answers on Ryan’s website)

    • Lab 1 to Lab 6 = 60% overall course + Final Challenge
    • 2 types of models: Classification and Regression
    • Lab 1: Classification on Credit Scoring
    • Lab 2: Summary statistics, Regression on Automobile price data

  • DAT209x

    • No final exams, only labs.

  • Project is on AML

  • Go through Azure Machine Learning modules:

    • Datasets
    • Run History
